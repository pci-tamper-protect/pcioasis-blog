# Blog Categories

Categories are informed by PCI DSS requirements but oriented toward a developer audience.
They are not a 1:1 mapping — a single post may touch multiple PCI requirements.

---

## `secure-devops`
CI/CD pipeline security, credential-free deployments, supply chain hardening, build system integrity.
_PCI influence: Req 6 (secure systems development), Req 8 (identity & authentication)_

## `identity-and-access`
IAM patterns, Workload Identity Federation, OAuth, RBAC, least-privilege service accounts, zero-trust identity.
_PCI influence: Req 7 (restrict access), Req 8 (identify and authenticate)_

## `compliance-engineering`
Translating PCI DSS requirements into concrete engineering decisions. Auditor-friendly architectures, evidence generation, control mapping.
_PCI influence: Cross-cutting_

## `cloud-security`
GCP, Firebase, and multi-cloud security configurations. Security posture, org policy, resource hierarchy, network controls.
_PCI influence: Req 1 (network controls), Req 2 (secure configurations)_

## `threat-intel`
E-skimming attack patterns, adversary TTPs, threat landscape, real-world attack case studies.
_PCI influence: Req 11 (test security), Req 12 (org security policy)_

## `detection-and-response`
Monitoring pipelines, alerting, log integrity, incident response patterns, forensic readiness.
_PCI influence: Req 10 (log and monitor), Req 11 (test security systems)_
